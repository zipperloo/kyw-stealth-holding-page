Dear future member,

I sold my consumer business to L'Oréal. Suddenly I had real wealth — and absolutely no way to manage it.

My bank gave me a relationship manager who changed every eighteen months. My accountant sent quarterly reports three months late. My lawyer's firm charged by the hour to explain what my accountant had already explained. My tax advisor needed information that lived in my broker's system, which didn't talk to my bank's system, which had no idea about my property portfolio.

Every advisor had a piece of the puzzle. Nobody had the picture.

I wasn't looking for advice.
I was looking for a system.

So I built one.

Know Your Worth is the operating system I wished existed. Sign up like Netflix. Connect your banks — all 10,000+ of them. See everything: every entity, every currency, every asset class. Not just reports. Real bookkeeping. Real workflows. Real control.

The architecture is built from transactions up, not dashboards down. Every entry is double-booked. Every position is reconciled. It's the same rigour that powers institutional systems, packaged so a single individual — or a family — can use it without a team of analysts.

Then I added an AI that actually understands your financial picture. Ask it anything, in English, by chat or voice. "What's my USD exposure?" "Show me my rental income vs expenses." "When's my next capital call?" Instant, contextual answers from your data — not generic advice scraped from the internet.

Not a simplified consumer app.
Not an institutional terminal.
The right level of depth for people who want to understand their wealth.

Here's what we will never do.

We will never trade your assets. We will never give investment advice. We will never earn commissions on your decisions. We will never sell your data. There are no conflicts of interest. You pay a software fee. That's it.

Your existing advisors — your accountant, your lawyer, your wealth manager — they don't get replaced. They get upgraded. They connect to your system, see your data in real-time, and collaborate within a single source of truth. You stop being the messenger between professionals who should already be talking to each other.

Your data is yours. Every access is logged. Every employee faces financial penalties for violations. We built the kind of privacy we'd want for our own family.

I spent years in boardrooms and deal rooms. I've sat across the table from acquirers, auditors, and private bankers. And the one thing I kept coming back to was this: the people who manage the most complex personal balance sheets in the world have the worst tools to do it.

Spreadsheets. PDFs. Emails with attachments. Phone calls to ask for numbers that should already be on a screen.

That era is over.

I'm the first user of Know Your Worth.
I use it every day to manage my own wealth.
My family's future depends on this software working.

I'd love for you to try it.

Elliot
